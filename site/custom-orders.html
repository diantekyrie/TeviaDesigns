How to measure

Measure in the places listed below

Use these instructions before typing your numbers into the custom order form.

Chest / Bust

Wrap the tape around the fullest part of the chest or bust and keep it level across the back.

Waist

Measure around the natural waistline, usually the narrowest part above the hips.

Hips

Measure around the fullest part of the hips and seat while standing naturally.

Inseam

Measure from the top of the inner thigh down to the desired pant length.

Shoulder

Measure straight across from one shoulder point to the other across the upper back.

Sleeve

Measure from the shoulder point down the arm to the wrist or desired sleeve finish.
